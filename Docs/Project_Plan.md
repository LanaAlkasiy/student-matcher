# Student Matcher

## Overview

Student Matcher is an AI-powered career discovery platform designed to help students find internships, jobs, graduate programs, and training opportunities that match their skills, experience, and career goals.

Students can upload a CV or provide a LinkedIn profile, and the platform will analyze their profile, identify relevant opportunities, explain match scores, highlight missing skills, and provide recommendations for improving their career readiness.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often spend hours searching across multiple platforms such as LinkedIn, Indeed, company career portals, and university job boards.

Many students also struggle to understand:

* Which opportunities fit their profile
* Why they are or are not qualified
* Which skills they need to improve
* How to strengthen their CV

Student Matcher aims to solve these challenges through AI-powered matching and career guidance.

---

## Core Features (Version 1)

### User Profile

* Upload CV (PDF)
* Select location
* Select desired role

### Job Matching

* Match jobs and internships to user profile
* Display match percentage
* Explain why each role matches

### Skill Analysis

* Identify missing skills
* Recommend skills to learn
* Suggest CV improvements
